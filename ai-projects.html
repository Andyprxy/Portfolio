Business Intelligence Dashboard

Tech Stack: Python (Streamlit/Plotly) | Focus: Data Visualization

The Problem: "Data Blindness"

Small businesses often collect sales data but fail to gain insights from it. Without visualization, owners cannot identify peak sales hours, popular products, or revenue trends, leading to poor inventory management.

The Solution

I built a Custom BI Tool designed for SMEs to interact with their sales data in real-time. Unlike static Excel sheets, this dashboard is interactive and builds trust through transparency.

  • Dynamic Filtering: Users can slice data by date, product category, or region.
  • Visual Insights: Automated generation of Sales vs. Target charts and Heatmaps.

AI Sales Agent (WhatsApp)

Tech Stack: n8n, OpenAI, WhatsApp API | Focus: 24/7 Automation

The Problem: Missed Sales & Repetitive Tasks

Manual order taking via WhatsApp is time-consuming and prone to human error. Businesses lose revenue when they cannot respond immediately to queries after hours.

The Automation Solution

I developed an Autonomous AI Sales Agent that lives on WhatsApp. It acts as a 24/7 employee, handling customer interactions without human intervention.

How it works:
  1. Inquiry: Customer asks about products or prices on WhatsApp.
  2. AI Processing: The agent (powered by LLMs) understands intent and answers FAQs instantly.
  3. Order Taking: It collects order details and automatically logs them into a database.
